About Us
  • National Relief Fund (NRF) was established entirely with public contributions and does not get any budgetary support.
  • NRF accepts voluntary contributions from Individuals, Organizations, Trusts, Companies and Institutions etc.
  • The resources of the NRF are utilized to render immediate relief to families of those killed in natural calamities like floods, cyclones and earthquakes, etc.
  • Assists partially to defray the expenses for medical treatment like heart surgery, kidney transplantation, cancer treatment of needy people and acid attack etc.
